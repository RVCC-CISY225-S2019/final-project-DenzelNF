Image Hover Effect

A

picture can be worth a thousand words and with a image hover effect it can be worth even more. When a user hovers their cursor over a image it can do a variety of different things. It can make the image pop out, shrink in, shake, on this web page it has a transparency to it. This makes a decent picture become a interesting picture just by using CSS. The CSS code is actually predefined algorithms that enable you to add special effects to your images like the ones talked about before and much much more.

The concept of editing photos is a unique skill that can have a positive and/or negative effect to them. As a whole photo editing is a great tool to enhance an image or to make it more interesting. For example, city light can be the center focus of a piece and due to the city, it can come across as dull, maybe bleak. But enhancing those lights can make an old city turn in to a one that is young again. Photos can change so much it could make a person feel completely different or look different too, but we’ll get to that. Imagine New York City with every person needs to be somewhere and capture that moment at night. Seems cool but turn the photo black and white and add a color to the picture once you hover the cursor of it. Wouldn’t that have more to say than just a picture of New York?

Too much of photo editing can steer away from the original piece for example photoshop. You can practically put a toilet for my head and make it seem like I was born with it. Photoshop/editing is all over movies, and social media, people making their bodies slimmer, butts bigger and most popular teeth whiter. This just alters the person and their appearance and makes them look not like them. Of course, its fine to alter yourself on photoshop, but then calling it your true self afterwards isn’t right.

In other words, images but using an image hover effect can make it even better. Why settle for just average while you can add a little flair to your work of art.
